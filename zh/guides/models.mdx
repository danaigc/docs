## Available Models

Egg API provides access to multiple AI models for image and video generation.

## Pricing

All pricing is pay-as-you-go with no monthly minimums.

| Model | Type | Unit | Price |
|-------|------|------|-------|
| `nanobanana` | Image | per image | $0.01 |
| `nanobanana-pro` | Image | per image | $0.016 |

### Example Costs

| Use Case | Calculation | Cost |
|----------|-------------|------|
| 1 image (nanobanana-pro) | 1 × $0.016 | $0.016 |
| 4 images (nanobanana) | 4 × $0.01 | $0.04 |
| 10 images (nanobanana-pro) | 10 × $0.016 | $0.16 |

## Choosing the Right Model

| Use Case | Recommended Model |
|----------|-------------------|
| Quick prototyping | nanobanana |
| Production images | nanobanana-pro |
| Social media posts | nanobanana-pro |
| Marketing materials | nanobanana-pro |

## Billing

- Charges are applied immediately upon successful generation
- Failed generations are not charged
- Check your usage with `GET /v1/usage/summary`
- View your balance in the [dashboard](https://eggapi.ai/dashboard)
