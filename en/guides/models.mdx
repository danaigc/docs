## Available Models

Egg API provides access to multiple AI models for image and video generation.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="veo3.1" icon="video" href="/en/guides/models/veo3.1">
    **Video Generation**

    High-quality video generation with excellent motion consistency.

    - Async processing
    - 1-60 seconds duration
  </Card>
  <Card title="nanobanana" icon="image" href="/en/guides/models/nanobanana">
    **Image Generation**

    Fast, efficient image generation for general use cases.

    - Asynchronous
    - 16:9 or 9:16 (256-2048)
    - Multiple outputs
  </Card>
  <Card title="nanobanana-pro" icon="star" href="/en/guides/models/nanobanana-pro">
    **Image Generation Pro**

    Professional-grade image generation with enhanced detail.

    - Asynchronous
    - 16:9 or 9:16 (256-2048)
    - Higher quality
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Pricing

All pricing is pay-as-you-go with no monthly minimums.

| Model | Type | Unit | Price |
|-------|------|------|-------|
| `veo3.1` | Video | per second | $0.08 |
| `nanobanana` | Image | per image | $0.01 |
| `nanobanana-pro` | Image | per image | $0.016 |

### Example Costs

| Use Case | Calculation | Cost |
|----------|-------------|------|
| 1 image (nanobanana-pro) | 1 × $0.016 | $0.016 |
| 4 images (nanobanana) | 4 × $0.01 | $0.04 |
| 5-second video (veo3.1) | 5 × $0.08 | $0.40 |
| 30-second video (veo3.1) | 30 × $0.08 | $2.40 |

## Choosing the Right Model

| Use Case | Recommended Model |
|----------|-------------------|
| Quick prototyping | nanobanana |
| Production images | nanobanana-pro |
| Video content | veo3.1 |
| Social media posts | nanobanana-pro (images), veo3.1 (videos) |
| Marketing materials | nanobanana-pro |

## Billing

- Charges are applied immediately upon successful generation
- Failed generations are not charged
- Check your usage with `GET /v1/usage/summary`
- View your balance in the [dashboard](https://eggapi.ai/dashboard)
